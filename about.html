About the Faculty of Computer & Information Engineering

Learn about our mission, vision, and how we empower students to excel in technology and innovation.

Our Mission

The Faculty of Computer & Information Engineering at FUOYE aims to provide top-notch education and research opportunities in the field of computing and information technology. We focus on preparing students for both professional excellence and societal impact.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in computer and information engineering education, research, and innovation, contributing to technological advancement both nationally and globally.

What We Offer

  • High-quality undergraduate and postgraduate programs
  • Modern laboratories and research facilities
  • Strong collaboration with industry and technology partners
  • Active student association (CIESA) and vibrant community

Why Choose CIE at FUOYE?

Our faculty provides a balance of academic excellence, practical experience, and community engagement. Students gain hands-on knowledge, access to research projects, and opportunities to participate in national and international competitions.
