Departments in CIE

Explore the three departments that make up the Faculty of Computer & Information Engineering.

Computer Engineering (CPE)

Focuses on computer hardware, embedded systems, and integration of software and hardware solutions.

Systems Engineering (SYE)

Deals with the design, integration, and management of complex engineering systems.

Information & Communication Engineering (ICE)

Focuses on telecommunications, networking, and efficient transfer of information across systems.
